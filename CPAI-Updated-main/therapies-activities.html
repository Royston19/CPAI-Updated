Threrapies

Speech Therapy

People with different abilities (PDAs) may have major problems with both speech and nonverbal communication. They may also find it very hard to interact socially. For these reasons, speech therapy is a central part of the treatment for PDAs. Speech therapy can address a wide range of communication problems for PDAs.

Occupational Therapy

Occupational therapies can help kids with certain learning and attention issues in doing daily activities that they struggle to do.

Physiotherapy

At our center we provide detailed researched therapy. A prior assessment is done on day-one keeping in mind CBR (Community-Based Rehabilitation) aspect. Depending on assessment, appropriate therapy techniques are used by the therapist that includes Neuro developmental technique, Balance training, Fine motor training, Gross motor training.

Psychological Assessment Cum Counselling

This helps the people with mental disorders and intellectual disability. We do different forms of therapy such as behavioral therapy, expressive arts therapy etc. Assessment is done to screen the underline cause of the issue and the therapy is done thereafter.

Diet and Nutrition

Dietitians are responsible for the nutritional management of individuals who are referred to their care. It is well recognised that people with disabilities are at risk of nutritional problems, and therefore, it is very important that there is provision to dietetic services for this group.
